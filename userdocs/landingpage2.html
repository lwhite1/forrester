Shrewd

High-Leverage System Dynamics — A Courant Systems Engine

Complexity is a choice. Shrewd is an open-source modeling environment designed for those who need to move past "gut feelings" and uncover the mathematical skeleton of a problem. It provides a sharp, unvarnished view of how feedback loops drive behavior.

While legacy tools focus on the "boxes," Shrewd focuses on the dynamics. It is built for the architect who knows that the most dangerous part of a system is the part they didn't model.

Strategic Modeling

Immediate Feedback: Don't wait for a "run" button. Shrewd uses a real-time solver engine. As you adjust a stock or refine an inflow, the behavior curves update instantly. It turns modeling into an act of conversation with the system.

Modular Architecture: Complex models shouldn't be monoliths. Shrewd allows you to encapsulate logic into distinct modules with strictly defined ports. This isn't just "cleaner" modeling—it's professional-grade system design that allows for rapid scaling and collaborative building.

Analytical Depth

Most decisions fail at the thresholds. Shrewd’s built-in Monte Carlo engine and automated parameter sweeps allow you to stress-test your assumptions across thousands of futures. You aren't looking for a single forecast; you are looking for the robust path that survives volatility.

Optimization as Calibration: Shrewd includes a proprietary optimization layer that can "back-fit" your model to historical data, identifying the hidden parameter values that explain why the system behaved the way it did. It turns mystery into a solved equation.
