## Place two Stocks

Press `2` to switch to the Stock tool. Place two stocks on the canvas:

1. **Inventory** — initial value `200` (units in the warehouse)
2. **Supply Line** — initial value `0` (units on order but not yet delivered)

The Supply Line stock tracks orders that are in transit. This is important: you've placed orders but they haven't arrived yet. Ignoring the supply line is a classic cause of over-ordering.

Place Inventory on the left and Supply Line on the right.
