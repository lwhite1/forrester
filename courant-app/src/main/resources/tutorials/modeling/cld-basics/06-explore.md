## Extend the Diagram

A CLD is a thinking tool. Try extending your diagram with additional factors:

- Add a variable `Fuel Cost` and link it to Cars on Road with negative polarity — higher fuel cost means fewer drivers
- Add `Air Quality` linked from Congestion (negative) — more congestion degrades air quality
- Link Air Quality to Public Transit Use (negative) — worse air quality motivates people to use transit

Each addition may create new feedback loops. Use the loop navigator to discover them.

## From CLD to Stock-and-Flow

Once you are satisfied with the causal structure, you can convert individual CLD variables into formal stock-and-flow elements:

1. Press `1` to switch to the Select tool
2. Right-click a CLD variable
3. Choose **Classify as → Stock**, **Variable**, or another element type

This preserves the variable's position and name, and lets you add equations and units to build a runnable simulation.
