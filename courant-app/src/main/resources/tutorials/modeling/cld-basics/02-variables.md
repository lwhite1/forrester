## Place CLD Variables

Click the **Causal Variable** button in the toolbar to switch to the CLD Variable tool.

Click on the canvas to place six variables. Arrange them roughly in a circle so there is room for links between them. Double-click each one to name it:

1. `Cars on Road`
2. `Congestion`
3. `Travel Time`
4. `Public Transit Use`
5. `Road Construction`
6. `Road Capacity`

**Tip:** CLD variables are qualitative — they have no equations or units. Their purpose is to capture the causal structure of the system before committing to numbers.
